  • COMMEMORATION OF FATHER KINO
    February - April 2011
    Father Kino was sent by the Catholic Church to Arizona, Mexico and Baja California to convert local population to the Christian religion. This year marks the 300th Anniversary of his death and numerous events will take place to honor his work.
    For more information visit: www.padrekino.com.
  • SONS OF ITALY PICNIC
    March 27, 2011 from 11:30am to 3:30pm
    Bilotti Lodge Sons will host a picnic at Red Mountain Park. This is an occasion for everybody to enjoy Italian food, Italian Trivia, children's games, walking trails. Tickets are $10 for adults and $6 for children.
    For more information please call 480-986-9774.
