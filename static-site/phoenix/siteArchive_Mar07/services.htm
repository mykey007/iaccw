The objective of the Phoenix Chapter of the Italy-America Chamber of Commerce is to promote and facilitate the exchange between businesses and individuals in both Italy and Arizona. The Italy-America Chamber of Commerce West - Phoenix Chapter offers the following services to members and nonmembers. To our members, services are provided at a discount rate.

Information activities and services on:
»Fairs and exhibitions in the country
»Countries/sectors/markets
»Guided search of names from database and locating partners
»Business opportunities
»Thematic seminars and training cycles carried out in the country also by other subjects
»Financial instruments for internationalization
»International tenders

Promotional activities and locating partners
»Organization of missions to Italy
»Participation in trade fairs
»Assistance to economic missions on a visit to Italy
»Organization of bilateral entrepreneurial meetings
»Preparation of agenda for business meeting
»Locating trading partners and partners for joint ventures
»Circulation of requests for and offers of goods and services
»Mail-out activity
»Direct marketing

Training activities
»Training courses for firms
»Italian language courses
Other activities
»Translation
»Interpreter services

Networking opportunitiesThe Italy-America Chamber of Commerce West – Phoenix Chapter is keen on helping to cultivate business relations between both Italy and Arizona. For this purpose, we will be providing an array of networking activities to our members, giving them opportunities to get to know one another, to build and enrich their contacts on both a business and personal level. Professionals interested in or actively seeking exposure and new alliances or professional opportunities in the Arizona or Italian market have definitely much to gain through participation in these events. We will hold small informal group setting activities, where attendees bond and exchange business cards. All of our networking meetings are open to IACCW members, their guests plus other participants affiliated with related Chamber of Commerce organizations and other professional associations. In the future we plan to offer Videoconferencing Services
