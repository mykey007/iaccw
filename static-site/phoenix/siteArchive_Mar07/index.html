IACCW extend services to the Valley of the Sun

We are pleased to align ourselves with the Italy America Chamber of Commerce West (IACCW) in Los Angeles to extend services to the rapidly growing Valley of the Sun. The Phoenix Metropolitan area is now the sixth largest city in the U.S. with a population of approximately 3.5 million. No longer merely a land of cowboys and cactus, the Valley of the Sun is a dynamic metropolitan area geared to international commerce. The Phoenix area is also home to a very large population of Italian born citizens who have woven their lifestyle into the desert landscape thus spreading an interest for Italian design in clothing, home furnishings, architecture as well as fine wine and gourmet foods. Our mission is to foster all economic and trade relations between Arizona and Italy thus building a ponte (bridge) for an exchange of quality products and services that will greatly benefit both our economies. Our first priority is to provide our fellow partners and the community at large with a broad range of essential services, counseling/advisory activities, and assistance of an economic or commercial nature, as well as disseminate information to the general public with regard to our activities. Thanks to the close collaboration with several key organizations – in particular with the US Department of Commerce as well as the other chambers of commerce (local and Italian) and other associations in diverse sectors, the Italy-America Chamber of Commerce West – Phoenix Chapter is in a position to provide the vital services which fulfill the needs and demands of its own members while enabling businesses and individuals alike to access the information that they’re looking for and take advantage of all business opportunities existing between Italia and Arizona.

- Phoenix Chapter will be organizing events such as conferences, seminars, lunch meetings with high-profile attendees hailing from the entrepreneurial, political, institutional, diplomatic, and cultural domains – the goal being to foster communication among its own members, in hopes of finding common ground, social and business opportunities.

Through our affiliation with The Italy-America Chamber of Commerce West, we are a member of Assocamerestero , the world association of Italian bi-national chambers, and are affiliated with the EABA (European American Business Association). Moreover, IACCW collaborates with several key organizations – in particular with the US Department of Commerce – and other Chambers of Commerce, local and Italian.

Working closely with the representative office of Fiera Milano , one of the most important Exhibition centers in Europe , we are able to provide services such as Trade Show information, exhibition promotions and also organize buyers' delegations to several shows.
