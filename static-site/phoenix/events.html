Oltrepo Pavese Expo
The Italy-America Chamber of Commerce West – Phoenix Chapter will host the “Oltrepo’ Pavese Business Expo” in Scottsdale, Ariz., featuring businesses from the Lombardy region of Italy from April 16-22, 2007. The event will feature a variety of Italian businesses looking to expand their exports to Arizona. The participating businesses from Italy are part of a trade association called Associazione Terra Italia. Participants are all located in the Oltrepo’ Pavese area of northern Italy, near the fashion capitol and cultural city of Milan. Approximately 35 representatives of 24 different businesses are scheduled to participate in the expo.
